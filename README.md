# HR-Analytics-Dashboard-PowerBI
Interactive HR Analytics Dashboard built in Power BI to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, job satisfaction, salary distribution, and employee performance metrics. The dashboard provides actionable insights for HR decision making and workforce management.


# HR Analytics Dashboard

## Overview

This Power BI project provides comprehensive HR analytics insights to help organizations understand employee attrition patterns, workforce demographics, job satisfaction levels, and salary distribution.

The dashboard enables HR teams and management to identify key factors affecting employee retention and make data-driven workforce decisions.

---

## Dashboard Preview

Key business metrics are displayed through interactive visualizations including:

- Total Employees
- Attrition Count
- Attrition Rate
- Average Employee Age
- Average Salary
- Average Years at Company

---

## Key Insights

### Employee Attrition Analysis
- Overall attrition count and attrition rate
- Attrition distribution by age groups
- Attrition by years at company
- Attrition by salary slabs

### Workforce Demographics
- Employee distribution by education field
- Gender-based attrition analysis
- Department-wise employee insights

### Employee Satisfaction
- Job satisfaction analysis by job role
- Comparison of satisfaction ratings across departments

### Salary Analysis
- Attrition trends across different salary brackets
- Identification of high-risk employee segments
